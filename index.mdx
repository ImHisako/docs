## Get Started in 3 Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download Illegalcord">
    Head to the [Installation](/installation) page and download the installer for your operating system.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install and launch">
    Run the installer — Illegalcord injects into your existing Discord client automatically. Launch Discord as usual.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize your experience">
    Open **Settings → Illegalcord** to browse themes, enable plugins, and configure features to your liking.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Illegalcord runs alongside your existing Discord installation. Your account, servers, and messages are completely unaffected.
</Note>
