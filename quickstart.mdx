Once Illegalcord is installed, you're ready to make Discord your own. This guide walks you through the essential steps — applying a theme, enabling a plugin, setting up a keybind, and making sure everything stays up to date. You can complete the whole walkthrough in under five minutes.

<Note>
  All changes you make in the Illegalcord settings panel are saved automatically. There is no save button — just configure and go.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Illegalcord Settings">
    Click the **gear icon** (⚙️) in the bottom-left corner of Discord to open User Settings. Scroll past the standard Discord settings categories until you reach the **Illegalcord** section near the bottom of the sidebar. This section is your control center for everything Illegalcord offers.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Apply a Theme">
    Click the **Themes** tab inside the Illegalcord section.

    1. Click **Browse Themes** to open the community theme directory.
    2. Preview themes by hovering over them — Discord's interface updates in real time.
    3. When you find one you like, click **Install**, then toggle it on with the switch next to its name.

    You can install multiple themes and switch between them at any time. Only one theme is active at once unless you manually combine CSS.

    For a deeper look at creating and customizing themes, see the [Themes](/customization/themes) guide.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enable a Plugin">
    Click the **Plugins** tab inside the Illegalcord section.

    1. Use the search bar at the top to find a plugin by name or keyword.
    2. Click a plugin card to read its description, permissions, and author information.
    3. Toggle the switch on the right side of the plugin card to **enable** it. Some plugins show a small gear icon for additional settings — click it to configure them.

    <Tip>
      Not sure where to start? Here are three popular first plugins to try:

      - **BetterRoleColors** — applies server role colors to usernames throughout the entire UI, not just in the member list.
      - **MessageLogger** — keeps a local record of deleted and edited messages so you never miss what was said.
      - **SpotifyControls** — adds Spotify playback controls (play, pause, skip, volume) directly into the Discord bottom bar.
    </Tip>

    Explore the full plugin catalog and learn how to build your own in the [Plugins](/customization/plugins) guide.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Up a Keybind">
    Click the **Keybinds** tab inside the Illegalcord section.

    1. Click **Add Keybind** to open the keybind editor.
    2. Choose an **action** from the dropdown — for example, "Toggle Mute", "Run Plugin Command", or "Switch to Server".
    3. Click **Record Keybind** and press the key combination you want to assign.
    4. Click **Save**. The keybind is active immediately.

    You can add as many keybinds as you need and edit or delete them at any time from the same tab.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check for Updates">
    Click the **About** tab inside the Illegalcord section to see your current Illegalcord version and the version of Discord it's patched against.

    - If an update is available, an **Update Illegalcord** button appears at the top of the page. Click it to download and apply the latest release without leaving Discord.
    - If you're already on the latest version, the page displays a green **Up to date** badge.

    Illegalcord also checks for updates automatically every time Discord launches, so this step is usually just for peace of mind.
  </Step>
</Steps>
